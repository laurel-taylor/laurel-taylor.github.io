About Sophie's Universe

Sophie's Universe is the name of a crochet project that was made by the illustrious Dedri Uys of https://lookatwhatimade.net and was the CAL (crochet-along) project of 2015.

Please check out her site for all the information about the project: https://lookatwhatimade.net/crafts/yarn/crochet/sophies-universe-cal-2015/sophies-universe-cal-2015-information/

The pattern is FREE and there is SO MUCH helpful information on the site. Luckily, she kept track of the amount of yarn used for each round and I threw that data into this project.

About the calculator

This calculator was made to make it easier to get an idea of what it will look like when you choose your own colors, and purchase the right amount of yarn to make Sophie.

Obviously, your milage (meterage?) may vary.

When you press the "calculate skeins" button it will show you how many balls you will need, given the meterage of your skeins. Green means you'll probably be good, red means you might want to up your # of balls (or play a nice game of yarn chicken).

The default yarn color names are Scheepjes brand, and were the ones used in Dedri's original projects:

  • Small -> Pattern 1 -> Scheepjes Color 8
  • Medium -> Pattern 2 -> Scheepjes Softfun
  • Medium -> Pattern 3 -> Scheepjes Colour Crafter
  • Large -> Pattern 4 -> Scheepjes Softfun XLr

You can find links for where to buy the yarn on her site!
